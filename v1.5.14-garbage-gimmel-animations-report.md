# v1.5.14 Garbage / Gimmel Animations

Adds two approved production special pin animations.

## Registry entries

- `garbagebinlift` — Garbage Bin Lift, duration `6600` ms, draw wrapper `garbagebinliftv11DrawPin`.
- `gimmelthreedrums` — Gimmel Three Drums, duration `3400` ms, draw wrapper `gimmelthreedrumsv3DrawPin`.

## Source files reviewed

- `garbage-bin-lift-review-v11.html`
- `garbage-bin-lift-review-v11.txt`
- `gimmel-three-drums-review-v3.html`
- `gimmel-three-drums-review-v3.txt`

## Integration notes

- No earlier active `garbagebinlift` or `gimmelthreedrums` registry entries existed in `app-v1.5.13.js`, so the approved versions were added under the requested identifiers.
- Garbage Bin Lift v11 keeps the 6600 ms sequence, with truck departure beginning after the bin has returned, five tiny green bags dropping from the low side of the tipped rim, and the bin staying still while the truck drives off.
- Gimmel Three Drums v3 keeps left/right colored v2 drums and the middle original v1 drum. The later v4 drum animation was not used.
- The reusable drawing helpers are namespaced with `garbagebinliftv11` and `gimmelthreedrumsv3`.
- No gameplay rules, scoring, pin counts, startup overlay behavior, or external dependencies changed.
